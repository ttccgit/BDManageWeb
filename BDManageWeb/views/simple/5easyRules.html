5 Easy Rules of Simple Start

How to follow Simple Start, in 5 easy steps:

1Choose a meal. Eat it.

Simple Start meals are created from Vigilantes do Peso Power Foods; good-for-weight-loss ingredients that help fill you up faster.

Just pick from the 55 meals and snacks in the app, and start eating. (Feeling creative? Go to the My Meals section to build your own breakfast, lunch or dinner!)

2Don't worry about portions.

Really. By eating off the Simple Start Food List, you're consuming foods that help you fill up faster-and avoiding the foods that lead to overeating.

If you want guidance around portions, we've photographed our meals on a 10-inch plate, a 12-oz bowl and a 12-oz glass (look for them on the meal detail screens). These visual cues show you how much food each meal makes.

3Don't deprive yourself.

Feel like a glass of wine? Go for it. Craving a pat of real butter on your light English muffin? Enjoy. You can have 7 ProPontos® values per day (49 per week) to spend on indulgences. To make it simple, we've listed some of the foods that members frequently choose, and labeled them with their ProPontos values. Enjoy them on their own or as an ingredient in a meal.

4Have 2 tsp of healthy oils per day, total.

No, no, not in a shot glass. Cook with them, use them in a salad - whatever works for you. Although oils are not Power Foods, they do work well with this plan. Check the Simple Start Food List to see which ones to use.

5Choose unlimited "free" beverages.

Drink as much as you want of the following: Water, with or without bubbles (plain or flavored, unsweetened); coffee or tea (without sugar, fat-free milk only); diet soda.
