How It Works

Want More Choices?

If Simple Start is working for you, keep doing it! (It's based on our Simply Filling technique.)

If you want to branch out and look for other meals, though, or indulge in foods that aren't in the Simple Start Indulgences list, go to WeightWatchers.com or download the WeightWatchers Mobile app to learn more about Simply Filling.

If you'd like even more choice in the foods you eat...

Use the personalized daily ProPontos® Target we gave you when you joined, and spend those ProPontos® values however you want. Simply stick to that Target plus your weekly ProPontos® Allowance (still 49 per week); you'll have to track everything you eat and pay more attention to portion sizes, but you'll get more flexibility around your food choices.

Here's the thing, though: You'll quickly find that it's easier to stick to your daily ProPontos® Target - and to avoid feeling deprived while you're doing it - if you choose a lot of Vigilantes do Peso Power Foods. That's why we started you off with Express. We wanted you to experience the benefits of these foods.

Making the switch

Everything you've come to rely on won't just disappear. (Hey, you've learned to enjoy smart and satisfying foods!) And the meal lists? They live right on the Weight Watchers website. (You can also get them by downloading the Weight Watchers mobile app.)
