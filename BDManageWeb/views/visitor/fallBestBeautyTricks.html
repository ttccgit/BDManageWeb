As the weather cools, our wardrobes aren´t the only things that need revamping.

Article By: Mary Elizabeth Hurn

Nothing delivers a confidence boost faster than a pretty new polish or smooth, silky skin. On weeks that you´ve lost weight, it feels like a treat–and on weeks when you haven´t, getting an extra dose of "I feel pretty" will keep you motivated. This fall, as you swap your shorts for slacks and tank tops for trench coats, try our favorite tips for updating your beauty routine.

Go bold with bright lips
As the temperature drops, keep a little sizzle in your life with darker lips. Add a burst of brightness with a juicy magenta, vivid crimson or deep wine lipstick or stain. A vibrant lip color is also a great way to glam up comfy jeans and a fitted tee. Smart idea: get one that stays put, since you´ll probably be sipping more hot beverages this season.
Editors´ pick: Butter London´s Lippy Tinted Balm in Black Cherry, $20

Repair your hair
Chlorine, UV rays and summery highlights can wreak havoc on your locks. Reverse the damage with a deep conditioner or hair mask to keep your hair shiny and flyaway-free. It doesn´t hurt if it just happens to smell like a day spa....
Editors´ pick: John Masters Organics Honey & Hibiscus Hair Reconstructor, $28

Opt for darker polish
Corals and neons are SO last season. Try swapping them out for a vampy eggplant, pewter grey or midnight blue. But remember, chips are much more noticeable with darker colors, so find a polish or top coat that really sticks.
Editors´ pick: Treat yourself to a manicure with CND Vinylux Weekly Polish in Dark Dahlia,salons nationwide.

Maximize your eyes
For most of the country, beach and poolside days are (sadly) behind us, which means (happily) you can reach for your blackest mascara starting... now!
Editors´ pick: CoverGirl´s Clump Crusher Extensions LashBlast Mascara, $7

Moisturize, moisturize, moisturize
Chilly winds and drying indoor heat can lead to dry, chapped skin – never a good look. Protect yourself by switching to a slightly thicker moisturizer or night cream.
Editors´ pick: RoC´s Multi Correxion 5-in-1 Restoring Night Cream, $29

Spruce up your scent
When you pack away your sundresses and pull out the leggings, that´s the time to swap your scent. Trade lightweight, fruity summer fragrances for something more earthy, spicy or rich.
Editors´ Pick: L´Occitane´s Rose 4 Reines Eau de Toilette, $47

Exfoliate for smoothness
Sunburns and self-tanners can do a number on your face. Slough away unevenness with an exfoliator that buffs, smooths and moisturizes.
Editors´ Pick: Ole Henriksen´s 3-step micro/mini peel system, $95
