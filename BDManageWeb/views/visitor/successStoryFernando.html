Health... from father to daughter

By adopting a balanced diet, an active life and new habits, such as tracking behaviors, planning routines and practicing self-control, member Fernando Henrique said goodbye to 29 kg and now can consider himself a healthy father.

Learning in order to teach

When Fernando decided to join the program, he weighed 115 kg. Besides his intention to lose weight to have a better quality of life, he was also worried about his daughter’s health. He wanted her to eat healthily. “My inspiration came from the willing to be a model to my daughter,” he says.

New habits to succeed

After listening to his Leader and reading the program’s advice, Fernando started tracking. In his Tracker, he took daily notes of what he had eaten and which physical activities he had practiced. It helped him to evaluate his progress and find out what hadn’t worked for him during the weight-loss process. “Another crucial tool was the ProPoints Calculator. It became my ally to portion control,” he recalls.

Fernando used interesting strategies to control his portions and avoid excesses that could hinder his weight-loss. “I started eating my main meals from a dessert plate. In just four weeks I was already used to it and realized that what I used to eat was much more than I really needed to feel satisfied,” he says.

The Express Menus in the program helped Fernando to plan ahead and make smart food – without cutting off what he liked best. I’ve learnt how to control the treats I eat and avoid overindulgence. Now I think: ‘Should I eat this slice of pudding now or should I drink some wine and eat some cheese later, when I’m with my friends?’,” he reveals.

When motivation comes from the inside

Fernando has also become an optimistic person. He really believed he could succeed, and became his best friend during the weight-loss process. By being optimistic about his results, he suffered no influence from people who misjudged him or tried to hinder his weight-loss. “When somebody laughed about my choices or implied that I wouldn’t be able to reach my goal, I found the motivation I needed inside me. It was like being challenged,” he says.

Moving more to lose more

The physical activities also played an important role in Fernando’s success. He was already used to running three times a week, but he intensified his trainings and, now, he frequently joins 5 to 15k running events. “By the end of 2013, I want to run the São Silvestre’s International Race once more – only in 1h40. During my prep trainings, I try to accelerate to reduce the final time and to improve my physical conditioning.”

A model of a father

As he lost weight, Fernando Henrique noticed the influence parents have in their children’s eating habits. In the member’s opinion, most families are used to too many activities that include food, and that can lead to severe consequences to everybody’s health. “When I decided to lose weight, my daughter complained about losing her ‘pillow’. She’s just 8 years old and she is my greatest follower as far as eating and running habits are concerned”, he proudly says.

By, reaching his goal of 86 kg, Fernando felt just like an Olympic medalist and repeated to himself that he would never gain weight again. “It was a great pleasure when I threw away the pants that were too loose. They were substituted by old pants which I hadn’t used for about 10 years. I’ll keep my new lifestyle forever”, he concludes.
