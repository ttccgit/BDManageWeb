Physical and mental wellness to be happy as a couple

Luciana, a member from Minas Gerais, learnt how to manage the environment and built healthier habits that had a positive impact in her health, looks and marriage.

Time to change

Luciana was facing difficult times in her marriage. To feel better, she decided to become more attractive. She was 40 years old and weighed 83,2 kg when she joined Vigilantes do Peso. Motivated by the Success Stories she had read on our website, she looked for the nearest meeting.

When she started following the program, she learnt that, to achieve her goals, she would have to change her lifestyle by building new habits. “When I set goals in my life, even the hardest ones, I never give up. I committed to my goal, which was reaching a healthy weight, and I overcame many challenges”, she says.

Building new habits

After a week, Luciana could already notice some benefits of making smarter food choices: she had lost weight.

As the program recommended, she changed the environment so that it matched her new lifestyle: “I’ve substituted the caloric foods and filled the pantry and the fridge with healthier items”. In her menu, fries gave way to fresh fruits and vegetables.

Luciana started making smarter choices everywhere she went: “Now I always carry healthy snacks. It’s a way not to overeat in my main meals. I also take my own lunch to work. It’s always prepared with healthy ingredients.”

In five months, Luciana had already lost about 15 kg. It was then that she stopped losing weight. “To overcome it, I followed my Leader’s advice: I started walking in a regular basis. It helped me to achieve my goal of 67 kg”, she says.

Now, Luciana maintains her weight-loss and keeps attending the Vigilantes do Peso meetings. “I’m not a [Brazilian] size 46 anymore. I’m a 42! I feel more beautiful and more confident in my marriage. My coworkers and relatives have noticed the implications of my new lifestyle and felt motivated to build healthier habits to achieve wellness, too” she says.
