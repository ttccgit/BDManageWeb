“It’s great to hear from people that said that I was overweight that I’m more beautiful now.”

She surprised everyone!

We have interviewed Roberta Simões, a member who’s lost more than 8 kg.

Vigilantes do Peso: Tell us, Roberta, why did you think that it was the time to join the program?

Roberta Simões: There were varied, but two of them must to be highlighted. First one: My clothes didn’t fit anymore, which was news to me. Second one: some relatives were saying I was overweight and asking what had happened to me. We didn’t live in the same state, and because of that, my new weight called their attention in our family’s meetings.

V.P.: So they were happy to know that you had joined Vigilantes do Peso, weren’t they?

R. S.: Yes (the ones that knew about it). I didn’t tell everybody because I didn’t want to create expectations, I wanted to see their reactions to my new weight.

V.P.: How was your first meeting?

R. S.: I wasn’t sure the program really worked. There are too many misleading ads as far as weight loss is concerned, but I decided to give it a try. While weighing was being carried on, I talked to some of the members, who really motivated me. I decided to pay for one month in advance, not to run the risk of giving up.

V.P.: What did you learn while you followed the program?

R. S.: I learned to prepare healthier meals. I started eating more vegetables, fruits and drinking more water. Now I can manage my environment and eat smarter. The best of all things is that I have a great life, I can go out and eat everything I want.

V.P.: You talked about the changes in eating habits. What about physical activities?

R. S.: They changed a little bit, too. Nowadays, I try to do everything to move more and I also started walking every day.

V.P.: Did you ever thought about giving up? What made you change your mind?

R. S.: Yes, sometimes. However, the wish of having a healthy weight, a good physical condition and my determination prevented me from giving up. So did the clothes I wanted to buy!

V.P.: When we face challenges it is important to have someone’s support. During your weight loss journey, could you count on someone?

R. S.: I could count on my husband, who always encouraged me. It was funny because whenever I ate something, he asked: “Can you eat that? How many POINTS values is it worth? Do you still have POINTS values to spend? Have you already used your PontosFlex?” In the beginning, he didn’t believe the program worked, he even promised if it worked, he would pay for the meetings. But when he could see the results, he was surprised. We also started opting for restaurants which offered more choices. We prefer the so-called “self-service restaurants”.

V.P.: How do you feel after having achieved your weight goal?

R. S.: It’s great to hear from people that said that I was overweight that I’m more beautiful now. They ask me what I have done to improve my looks. I’m very happy and excited. Looking back, I can’t believe I didn’t take care of myself and put on so much weight. I was always tired and couldn’t wear the clothes I wanted.
