“Food won't solve my problems.”

A healthy mind in a healthy body

After having her health conditions improved, and having lost 18,2 kg, member Andressa Alencar celebrates her achievements and shares the experiences of a new lifestyle.

Changing to the best

Weighing 73 kg, Andressa decided it was time to lose weight and recover her self-esteem. “One day I got tired. I got tired of being overweight. I got tired of buying ugly clothes – because they were the only ones that fitted me. I got tired of ‘avoiding’ myself. I got tired of eating to feel better. I got tired of being avoided by the one who had been my husband so far. I realized I needed to love myself.”

Andressa’s problems related to weight went beyond good looks. She was having health problems. “My knees hurt and, even though I live just on the second floor, climbing up the stairs was a sacrifice. I got home tired, my legs hurt and I was breathless.” So, she joined Vigilantes do Peso, put herself in first place, and changed her life.

Healthier eating habits

At the weekly meetings, Andressa got the information she needed and got inspired to eat smarter by the hints, articles and recipes published in Vigilantes do Peso Weekly. “I jumped into the program. I started preparing lighter snacks and meals, full of fruits and veggies, and now I also drink plenty of water. My body just asks for healthy foods.”

Gradually, Andressa also learnt how to control her portion sizes so that she wouldn’t overcome the Daily ProPoints target suggested by the program. “I cut down on the portions of almost everything I used to eat, and I was surprised to notice that I wasn’t hungry! I made smart choices to maintain a varied and flexible menu. By doing that, I managed to lose weight by eating small amounts of my favorite foods, such as pasta, sweets, risotto and red meat.”

Tracking to succeed

As she was losing weight, Andressa had her Tracker as an ally to reach her goals. “My notes helped me to evaluate what I ate, the ProPontos values I used, and the emotions and attitudes that affected my weight-loss. This tool has showed me that food won’t solve my problems.”

Vida nova

Now weighing 54,8 kg, Andressa celebrates both her physical and mental health. “Now that I have a lighter body, I have more energy, I got rid of my blood circulation problems and my legs don’t hurt anymore. The results of my clinical exams have never been so great. And, as a consequence of my healthy eating, my skin and my hair are shiny.”

Andressa says that now she feels like she’s able to do anything she wants. “Every time I look at the mirror and I see myself in a [Brazilian] size 38 pair of pants, I feel happy and proud of myself for having followed the program and having lost weight without giving up on having a social life. Nowadays I date this special guy, I travel when I’m on holidays and I enjoy parties, weddings and birthdays while I maintain my weight without privation.”
