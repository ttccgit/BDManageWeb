Every food and drink has a ProPontos ® value, based on its nutritional makeup — protein, fiber, fat and carbs. What you eat and drink each day should add up to your daily ProPontos Target, which is determined by your age, height, weight and gender.

You can eat whatever you like, and a great place to start is by figuring out the ProPontos values of the foods you regularly eat in yourPointsPlus Tracker. You can settle for the result as is, or try these tricks for getting a great bang for your buck:

1. Play with portion sizes — you can adjust these in the ProPontos Tracker.
2. Find a lower ProPontos value version of what you want to eat.
3. Pick an alternative, low-ProPontos value food.

It’s also a good idea to focus on Power Foods, which we’ve determined will help you stay satisfied longer and deliver more nutrients for the ProPontos value. They’re identified by green triangles in our database.

It's important to fit all food groups into your Target each day. That will give you better nutrition and much-needed variety. You can distribute it however you like, but here are a few suggestions for various Targets.

You’ll notice that fresh fruits and vegetables, have 0 ProPontos values. They’re such healthy, filling choices that we want to encourage you to eat them. Consider them prepaid! Some condiments and small portions of other foods have 0 ProPontos values, too.

All fresh fruit and most vegetables have been assigned ProPontos values of 0. We’re assuming you’re going to eat a reasonable amount of these, and that’s already factored into your daily ProPontos Target.
