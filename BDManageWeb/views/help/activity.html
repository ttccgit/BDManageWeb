  • Activity

Physical activity can help you lose weight, is important for keeping it off, and can also help improve your health. Our Activity Assessment tool can give you a weekly activity ProPontos® goal that will help you see these benefits. Your aim is to work up to earning 4-6 activity ProPontos values per day.

You don’t have to work out at the gym to get the physical activity you need. Any kind of movement is good for you – from walking around the block to everyday activities like gardening. Walking is one of the simplest and best exercises to build on.

Read our safety tips before starting. Whatever activity you do, we want you to start slowly and build up gradually. You should always talk to your doctor before starting any exercise program. Do not exercise without professional supervision if you’re more than 30 pounds overweight.

To find the ProPontos value of an activity, search for them by name in the Activity Tracker, or use the activity ProPontos Calculator.

The activity ProPontos values you earn are based on your current weight, the time you spend moving, and the intensity of your efforts. Be realistic about your intensity level so you don't overestimate the number of ProPontos values you earn.

The ProPontos Tracker will by default swap the activity ProPontos values you’ve earned for food ProPontos values if you’ve used up your weekly ProPontos Allowance. (If you prefer to use your activity ProPontos values before your weekly ProPontos Allowance, you can change this by going to your Settings.)
