  • Weekly ProPontos® Allowance

Simply, you get an extra 49 ProPontos® values per week, to give you some flexibility with the ProPontos system, and help you enjoy the foods you love. You don’t have to use it, but we hope you will. And you can still lose weight, even if you use it all.

There’s no restriction on the kinds of food and drink you use it on. You can use it all at once during the week (say, at a party), or little by little throughout the week (say, for a daily dessert). If you want the flexibility of the weekly ProPontos Allowance but don’t want to spend as many as 49 extra ProPontos values, you can adjust your Allowance in the settings section of our mobile apps.
