Health and quality of life at work

Natalia Geara was already a Vigilantes to Peso member when she took the program to her workplace (Vigilantes do Peso At Work), acquired healthier habits and adapted the environment where she spent most of her time in order to eat healthily and lose weight successfully.

A new weight for a new life

Natália was engaged and, weighing 75.9 kg, she found in her wedding the motivation she needed to lose weight and conquer a healthier lifestyle. “My initial goal was to lose 5 kg, because, just like every woman does, I wanted to be gorgeous in my wedding gown on the most special day of my life,” she recalls. So, Natalia decided to join Vigilantes do Peso.

At the meetings, she counted on the group support to put the program into practice. “My Leader and the other member’s encouragement was crucial to the development of my new habits. From them, I learnt that it was possible to lose weight without feeling deprived.”

Meetings at her workplace

As Natália lost weight, she was always praised by her coworkers and everybody wanted to know her secret. By searching Vigilantes do Peso website, she learnt that it was possible to take the meetings to her workplace by means of Vigilantes do Peso Empresas.

Natália says that she carried out an informal research to know who would be interested in joining the program, and talked to her manager about the idea. The board approved the partnership with Vigilantes do peso and made a room available for the meetings. “With the At Work program, even the employees who didn’t have much available time were benefited, because the meetings happened during the work time,” she says.

Smart eating choices

Natália started to attend the meetings in the company, and there she got more hints on how to keep the healthy habits she had already developed and how to make even smarter eating choices. “For lunch, I opted for lighter foods, such as salads and grilled fish, and I got used to eating fruits and other healthy snacks between the meals,” she says.

At the meetings, she learnt how to plan ahead to eat smart when she traveled on business. “In a thermal bag, I always kept some healthy snacks, such as fruits, whole bread and white cheese,” she recalls. She also learnt to say “no” when her coworkers offered some food she hadn’t planned to eat.

By controlling what and how much she ate, Natália noticed that she could eat her favorite treats and still lose weight. “I used to open a packet of cookies and eat it up all by myself, without savoring it. I ate compulsively and then I regretted having done that. Nowadays I chew slowly so that I can feel the flavor of what I’m eating, and I also pay attention to my body’s satisfaction signs not to overeat.”

Winning Results

As she followed the program, Natália said good-bye to 18.7 kg. “The best sensation of my life was when I tried on a pair of [Brazilian] size 42 shorts (I used to wear 46). I felt I was beautiful, powerful, able to control my life and my weight. I recommend the program to all that want to lose or maintain their weight.”
